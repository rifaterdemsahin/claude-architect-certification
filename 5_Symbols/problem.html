❓ The Core Problem

There is no standardised certification path for architects who need to prove competency in Claude AI at the enterprise level. Professionals face three compounding challenges:

  1. 1. No clear learning path — Anthropic's documentation covers APIs but not enterprise architecture patterns, routing topologies, or multi-agent governance.
  2. 2. No proof of competency — Teams cannot distinguish between someone who "uses Claude" and someone who can design, audit, and defend a production-grade system.
  3. 3. No exam preparation resource — The Claude Certified Architect exam tests deep knowledge of ZDR, MCP security, prompt caching, and multi-agent design — topics with no consolidated study guide.
