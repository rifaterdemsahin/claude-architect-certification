## 🎯 Purpose

External consulting notes, architectural advice, and stakeholder recommendations that arose during the project and shaped design decisions.

These are distinct from error logs and workarounds — they capture deliberate architectural choices influenced by consulting sessions or external expertise.

## 📄 Files

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `architecture_consulting.md` | Architecture consultation notes and recommendations for enterprise AI integration patterns |

## 📐 What Belongs Here

- Notes from architecture review sessions
- External expert recommendations that drove a design decision
- Stakeholder feedback with structural impact (not bug reports — those go in `errors/`)

## 🚫 What Does NOT Belong Here

- Bug reports → `6_Semblance/errors/`
- Workarounds → `6_Semblance/logs/workarounds.md`
- General retrospectives → `6_Semblance/logs/lessons_learned.md`
